Our Services

Batteries

Batteries contain a number of heavy metals and toxic chemicals and disposing of them by the same process as regular household waste has raised concerns with the environment.

Recycling

We process and recycle your waste materials to reduce landfill impact.

Hazardous Waste

Handling and disposing of hazardous waste safely to protect communities.

E-Waste

Handling and disposal of e-waste, which includes discarded electrical and electronic devices

Plastic

Due to purposefully misleading symbols on plastic packaging and numerous technical hurdles, less than 10% of plastic has ever been recycled.

Organic

Organic wastes are often disposed of with other wastes in landfills or incinerators, but since they are biodegradable , some organic wastes are suitable for composting and land application.
